Noam Aqua

Noam aqua - A songwriter , Composer and Guitarist born in israel in the year 1998, started with the guitar at the age of 12, at those first years he was influenced by old school blues and rock such as Eric Clapton Radiohead, The beatles, Pink Floyd.
After a few years he started writing songs in the shape of israeli blues and soft rock, now a days he performs in local bars drawing the crowd towards him with heartfelt melodies and leading dancefloors with groovy vibes.
